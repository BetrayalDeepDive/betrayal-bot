## Channel Voice Design and Script Prompt Systems

- To effectively convey complex information, such as exposing a scandal or revealing a hidden truth, it's crucial to set an immersive tone and use descriptive language to engage the audience, as seen in the use of sensory details like 'smells of old coffee and dust' and 'the lights hum a low note', which helps to create a narrative that captures the listener's attention.
- The use of a 'normal operating procedure' with regular audits and reports can be an effective disguise for illicit activities, as it allows them to blend in with legitimate entities and evade detection by creating a false sense of transparency and compliance.
